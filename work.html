Cellular Agriculture starts from a deep respect and
understanding of nature's magic
We are here to protect and multiply it,
not hack, modify, or destroy it.

CAS isn't in it for the science alone. We
don't obsess over petri dishes and labs.
Our purpose is to grow widespread
understanding of Cellular Agriculture. Our
projects help people imagine what a
future with Cellular Agriculture could look
and feel like.

The three pillars of CAS

Education

Helping people understand the intricacies of Cellular Agriculture.

Collaboration

Facilitating partnerships that further awareness of Cellular Agriculture.

Visioning

Thinking ahead to envision new opportunities and possibilities for people to experience a future with Cellular Agriculture.
